Introducing Icoaen Font.

A handcrafted icon font for academic excellence

Built for reuse and contribution.

Icoaen Font is an extention to the Font Awesome framework, so contribution is supported and standards are kept high. Managed through GitHub so design and development progress and tracking are clean and clear.

Used in the Open Academic Environment.

The Open Academic Environment is a unique system, so Icoaen Font helps clarify the capability and unify a langage to simplify complexity and leverage better interaction.

Academically focused and proven in the field.

A clear visual langage that helps us work through our academic lives, effcientlty and effectively. Researched and tested by students, libararians, researchers and instuctors all around the world.
